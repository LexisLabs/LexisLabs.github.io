Co-Lab

Ars Electronica Exhibition

Co-Lab is a project inspired by the idea of change through collaboration. Through playful engagement with conductive elements, the audience is able to explore mathematical patterns that are personified through sound. This installation explores human connectivity through touch and social interaction. Co-Lab responds to audience inputs in real-time creating a dynamic electronic soundscape.
